Problem Statement

Our checkout process had a 68% abandonment rate, with users dropping off primarily at the payment information step. User research revealed that the form was too long, lacked clear progress indicators, and didn't save information between sessions.

üí°

Solution Approach

We redesigned the checkout flow with a multi-step approach, implemented auto-save functionality, and added progress indicators. We also integrated one-click payment options and simplified the form fields to only essential information.

üîÑ

Process/Methodology

1. Research Phase: Conducted user interviews and analyzed analytics data to identify pain points.

2. Design Phase: Created wireframes and prototypes for the new multi-step flow.

3. Testing Phase: Ran A/B tests with 10,000 users to validate the new design.

4. Implementation: Worked with engineering to build and launch the new checkout experience.

The new checkout flow resulted in a 42% reduction in abandonment rate and increased conversion by 28%. Users completed checkout 2.3x faster on average, leading to $2.1M in additional revenue in the first quarter.
